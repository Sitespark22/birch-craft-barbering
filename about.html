Our Story

Built by a Barber, for Barbers.

SJ Sarah Jane Birch Owner & Master Barber
The Founder

Sarah Jane Birch

Before she ever picked up a pair of clippers, Sarah Jane Birch was a musician. She started playing at 17 — a background that gave her a feel for rhythm, detail, and the kind of patience that separates good technique from great craft.

She trained at Taylor Andrews Academy, one of Utah's most respected cosmetology and barbering programs, and brought with her a relentless drive to get it right. Growing up with six brothers gave her an early and thorough education in men's hair — the kind you don't get in a textbook.

On June 1, 2023, she opened the doors to Birch Craft Barbering at 795 E St. George Blvd. From day one, her vision was larger than her own chair: she wanted to build a shop where skilled barbers could grow, earn a real living, and take pride in their work.

That philosophy is baked into the culture of the shop. Sarah doesn't run Birch Craft for her own benefit — she runs it as a platform for her team, her clients, and her community.

"I want to do whatever I can to help these guys, not just have them benefit me."
Sarah Jane Birch — Owner & Master Barber, Birch Craft Barbering

Her specialties span skin fades, classic cuts, traditional straight razor shaves, and all hair types — a versatility that comes from genuine curiosity about the craft and years of dedicated practice.

What We Stand For

The Three Pillars

01

Craft

Every barber at Birch Craft is here because they take the work seriously. We hold a high standard — not to impress, but because your hair deserves it. Techniques are practiced, not improvised. Results are consistent, not lucky.

02

Consistency

The best barbershops are defined by repeat clients who come back because the cut is always right. We work to earn that trust every single appointment. Same quality, same care — every time you sit in the chair.

03

Community

Birch Craft is part of St. George. We serve locals, Utah Tech students, and visitors from Zion and Snow Canyon. Our barbers live here. This shop belongs to this community — and we take that responsibility seriously.

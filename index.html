Our Story

Craft Over Convenience

Birch Craft Barbering was founded by Sarah Jane Birch on June 1, 2023 — a musician-turned-master-barber who trained at Taylor Andrews Academy and built this shop as a place where craft comes first and every barber on the team is set up to thrive.

This isn't a franchise or a factory. It's a living shop rooted in St. George, built on the belief that the right haircut changes how you carry yourself.
